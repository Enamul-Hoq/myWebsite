Welcome to My Digital Space!

Greetings! I am Md. Enamul Hoq, a dedicated and passionate researcher in Biomedical Informatics and Imaging Informatics. With a multifaceted background encompassing sensor engineering, physics, and medical imaging, I am driven by the desire to innovate and contribute to the future of healthcare technology.

My journey has taken me through diverse experiences in quantitative imaging, machine learning, and advanced deep neural networks in radiology. At the intersection of technology and healthcare, I have honed my expertise to enhance diagnostic precision and patient care through cutting-edge research and application.

On this site, you will find a comprehensive showcase of my professional journey, including my resume, a portfolio of my work, links to my Google Scholar publications, ResearchGate profile, and updates on my latest projects. I also share moments from conferences and other professional events, highlighting the vibrant community that inspires and collaborates with me.

Thank you for visiting! Explore, connect, and feel free to reach out for collaborations or inquiries.
