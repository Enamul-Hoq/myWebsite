Welcome to My Digital Space!

Greetings! I am Md. Enamul Hoq, a dedicated and passionate researcher in Biomedical Informatics and Imaging Informatics. With a background in sensor engineering, physics, and medical imaging, I am driven to innovate and contribute to healthcare technology.
