Welcome to My Digital Space!

Greetings! I am Md. Enamul Hoq, a dedicated and passionate researcher in Biomedical Informatics and Imaging Informatics. With a multifaceted background in sensor engineering, physics, and medical imaging, I am driven to innovate and contribute to the future of healthcare technology.

Throughout my journey, I have worked on diverse projects involving quantitative imaging, machine learning, and advanced deep neural networks in radiology. My aim is to enhance diagnostic precision and improve patient care through cutting-edge technology. Explore, connect, and feel free to reach out for collaborations!
